About Rolex

Rolex is a Swiss luxury watch brand and manufacturer, known for its high-quality, durable, and iconic designs, with headquarters in Geneva and a history rooted in the pursuit of precision and innovation.

The name "Rolex" doesn't have a specific, official meaning, but according to company lore, the name was chosen for its short, easy-to-pronounce, and aesthetically pleasing qualities, with founder Hans Wilsdorf claiming a "genie whispered 'Rolex' in my ear".
